Ward Grove Christmas Tree Farm

Join us at Ward Grove Christmas Tree Farm as your family builds memories that will last for years. Beautifully shaped White Pine and Virginia Pine up to 14 feet tall are grown and cared for on a family farm where you can choose the perfect tree.

On weekends, your family can enjoy riding in a mule-driven wagon to select your tree. We will cut your tree, shake the needles and bale it for easy handling. When you have made your selection come back to the log cabin where complimentary hot chocolate and cider are provided. All children receive a candy cane as well as a coloring book.
